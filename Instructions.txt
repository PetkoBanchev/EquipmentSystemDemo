Instructions
Open EquipmentSystemDemo.exe in the Builds folder

Controls

Walk with WASD
Jump with space

Left click - Use item in left hand

Right click - Use item in right hand

E - equip Left hand / Interact with objects 

Shift + E - equip Right hand

Q - Unequip Left hand

Shift + Q - Unequip Right hand

H - Unequip head

T - toggle gun mode

C - toggle cursor visibility
